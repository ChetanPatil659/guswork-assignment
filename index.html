Frequently Asked Questions

HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) pipe is a flexible plastic pipe used for fluid and gas transfer. It is designed to convey potable water, slurries, gases, and chemicals, making it ideal for infrastructure, agriculture, mining, and industrial applications.

HDPE pipes offer superior corrosion resistance, flexibility, lower weight, longer lifespan (50+ years), and significantly reduced maintenance costs compared to traditional steel or iron pipes.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality throughout every production stage.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

PE100 grade polyethylene with optimal molecular weight distribution is carefully selected and tested to meet our stringent quality requirements before entering the production line.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
  • Strict material certification checks

Precision Extrusion

Raw material is fed into high-performance extruders operating at controlled temperatures, forming the pipe profile with exact dimensions and uniform wall thickness.

  • Controlled temperature extrusion
  • Uniform wall thickness
  • Continuous profile monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while the pipe is gradually cooled to stabilize its shape and relieve internal stresses for dimensional accuracy.

  • Vacuum sizing tanks
  • Precise outer diameter control
  • Stress-relief cooling cycle

Precision Sizing

Haul-off machines maintain constant pipe speed and tension, ensuring dimensional accuracy and consistent SDR (Standard Dimension Ratio) throughout every coil.

  • Constant haul-off speed control
  • SDR accuracy verification
  • Real-time dimension monitoring

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes comprehensive hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and material property verification against IS 5984, ISO 4427, and ASTM D3035 standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Full dimensional inspection
  • Third-party certification

Permanent Marking

Each pipe is permanently marked with diameter, pressure rating, material grade, production date, and certification marks for complete traceability throughout its service life.

  • Permanent ink-jet marking
  • Full traceability data
  • Certification mark application

Precision Cutting

Automated cutting machines produce pipes and coils to exact required lengths with clean, square cuts that ensure leak-free joints during installation.

  • Automated length control
  • Clean square cuts
  • Customizable lengths available

Protective Packaging

Finished pipes are carefully bundled, coiled, or palletized with protective wrapping to prevent damage during transport and storage, ready for delivery across India and globally.

  • Protective wrapping
  • Safe bundling & palletizing
  • Export-ready packaging
